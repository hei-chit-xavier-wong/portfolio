More about me

Prior to this point, I've never touched coding before but I was always interested in learning CSS and HTML, and this proved to be the perfect opportunity for me to not only start on my journey, but also serve as a practical space to exemplify my work. I hope to continue updating and improving this website as I learn how to code and animate new things!

I'm a Sociology undergraduate at the University of British Columbia, and I have an undying passion to learn and take on new challenges as I really enjoy the process of trying something new, failing at it, then overcome these obstacles. With the ever growing importance of tech, I believe that by expanding my skillset within the industry can help me gain more insight on how different components of life, whether business, health, or education intersect with tech. I might not be the most proficient at this yet, but I am completely commited to continue my learning and hopefully to build something special one day.

On a more personal note...

In my free time you can probably catch me trying a new restaurant (I really. Like really like food.), and baking or cooking a new recipe. When I'm not stuffing my face with food (rarely, but it does happen), I like to binge watch movies, TV shows, and play board games with friends (particularly fast paced games like Exploding Kitten, and social deception games like Coup). As someone who's been in Canada for a while, I also dabble in the mandatory skiing every year (albeit very badly). Aside from that, I like to go to the gym and play cozy video games like Stardew Valley and Fileds of Mistria, as well as more action-oriented games like Risk of Rain 2 and Enshrouded.
